Three roles, one shared live state

This working demo shows the core idea of the product: the guest assistant, the owner dashboard, and the staff feed all read and write the same live menu data (stored in your browser). Change something as the owner and the assistant's answers change instantly. That is what "AI grounded to the live menu" means in practice.

Guest assistant

A phone-framed chat that answers from tonight's live menu: quick replies, free text, smart pairings that lift the check with exact prices, and a full EN / ES / AR toggle with real right-to-left Arabic.

Try this, 60 seconds

  1. Open the Guest assistant and tap "Is the salmon available?" It is, and the assistant adds a smart pairing with the exact prices, the check-lift moment from the brief.
  2. Open the Owner dashboard and flip the switch to 86 the Grilled Atlantic Salmon. A reminder appears on the daily priorities board automatically.
  3. Back in Guest, ask again. Now it declines the salmon and suggests another main, at its live price.
  4. In Owner, change a price. Ask the guest assistant for a recommendation and it quotes the new price.
  5. Open the Staff feed. Your 86 and price changes appear there as alerts, alongside the simulated floor activity.
  6. Back in Owner, use the multi-tenant preview to switch to Blue Fig Bistro. None of Harbor & Vine's data is visible there, that is the per-restaurant isolation the product enforces with row-level security.
  7. Tip: open Owner and Guest in two browser tabs side by side. Changes sync across tabs live.
